PAGE DESIGN

Bring fordwards

(Traer adelante)

From Organize.

It makes something go fordwards in the document.

Carry backwards

(Enviar atrás)

From Organize.

It makes something go backwards in the document.

Selection panel

(Panel de selección)

From Organize.

Used to select objects and change their order or visibility.

Align

(Alinear)

From Organize.

Used to change the position of the objects. It also allows to show or hide the lines of the page.

Group

(Agrupar)

From Organize.

Used to group two or more objects to move and assign a format to them in group.

Turn

(Girar)

From Organize.

It allows to turn objects of the document.
